About

Who We Are

We are a diverse family united by faith — intentional about worship, discipleship, and carrying light into our city.

01

Mission

Love God. Love people. Serve the city.

Everything we do flows from the grace we have received. We teach Scripture with clarity, worship with sincerity, and invest in relationships that last beyond Sunday.

02

Vision

A city renewed by hope.

To see lives restored, families strengthened, and communities awakened—raising generations rooted in faith, walking in purpose, and reflecting the love and power of Christ in every sphere of life.

Foundations

Core Values

Six commitments that keep our worship sincere, our relationships honest, and our service generous.

Truth

Teaching anchored in Scripture with humility and care.

Presence

Worship that honors God and invites hearts to rest.

Belonging

Community where names are known and burdens are shared.

Generosity

Time, resources, and compassion offered freely.

Excellence

Quiet attention to detail — not for show, but out of love.

Courage

Faith that steps toward hard conversations and holy change.

Leadership

Meet Our Leaders

Our pastors shepherd New Life City with humility, Scripture, and love for people; equipping the church to worship faithfully and serve our city with grace.

Bishop Randeep Mathews

Senior Pastor

Bishop Randeep Mathews provides spiritual oversight and biblical teaching that anchors our church in both Scripture and the leading of the Holy Spirit.

With a shepherd’s heart, he equips believers to live out their faith with integrity in their homes, workplaces, and communities, raising leaders who carry hope, truth, and purpose into every sphere of life.

Pastor Anushree Mathews

Co-Pastor

Pastor Anushree Mathews faithfully serves alongside the church in pastoral ministry, nurturing a culture of heartfelt worship,strong discipleship, and genuine community.

Her passion is to see people know Christ deeply, grow steadily in their walk of faith, and experience a church that welcomes every soul with warmth, grace, and open arms.
